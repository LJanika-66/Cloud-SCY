Precise and Reliable Weather Forecast - Anywhere, Anytime

Welcome to CLOUD SCY, where you can find the latest and most accurate weather information from any corner of the world. Whether it’s daily forecasts or the analysis of special meteorological data, our app provides all the information you need!

Main Features:

  • Global Weather Data - Current and future weather for thousands of cities at the click of a button.
  • Detailed Forecasts - Temperature, precipitation, wind direction, humidity, and other meteorological data.
  • Light and Dark Modes - Choose the display mode that’s most comfortable for you.
  • User-friendly Help - If you get stuck, detailed assistance is available for every feature.
  • Error Reporting - With your feedback, you contribute to the continuous improvement of the app.

Your Weather Helpers

Meet Borakku Shinkō, Usagi Tomoko, and Hiyori Awai, our app's friendly yet shy mascots! They will help you navigate through the weather data, give tips, and guide you through the features in a friendly way.

Stay up to date, plan ahead, and make the most of every day with Cloud SCY!
